400 Forward
will bring together African people from all over the world!

400 Forward is a global movement for the reunification and renaissance of the African Diaspora. Beneficial partnerships and collaborations among peoples of African descent throughout the U.S., Caribbean and Europe will reinvigorate bridges to the African continent that will empower alliances in economics, politics and culture.

Why Attend?

400 Forward will leverage the skills, talents and resources that abound throughout the African Diaspora that historically have been exploited in carefully constructed systems of oppression and discrimination. 400 Forward will develop a 10-year strategic plan as the foundation for the African Diaspora to chart a new course of cultural self-determination, economic empowerment and political franchise with African countries.

Our unified work will lead to the cultivation of the next Century of African Renaissance.

What is 400?

A little over 400 hundred years ago, the first slave ship on the Atlantic (Western) Slave trade arrived in America. This ship joined countless others on the Indian (Eastern) Slave trade and many more that would partake in either of these trades as they boomed over the following years. Since then, the world has changed a lot. From African independence to the Civil Rights movements to an African-American president. We believe in continuing to move forward 400 years later.
